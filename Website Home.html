This is a minigame I made in Javascript. The goal is to guess what number is going to be displayed the next time you press the button. It is based on a regular 6-sided die, so it'll include the numbers 1 through 6. For example, the first click might give 4, the next might give 7, then 13, then 18 and so on. Basically, everytime you click the button, it'll add a random number from 1 to 6 to the current number. The game will stop if the number is higher than 100. You win if your guess matches the next number. Good luck and have fun.
